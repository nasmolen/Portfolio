Nick Smolen

Founder of ArtBot.tv and aspiring web developer

The Past

Ever since I watched Aladdin, I was obssessed with movies. Maybe it was my first exposure to a dark-skinned protagonist, but I really think it was Robin Williams's timeless perfomance that made such an impression. I remember watching the Pierce Brosnan Bond movies and thinking about how amazing it would be to be Pierce; not Bond. The Matrix changed everything. It was the first time I fully realized the powerful and transformative potential of movies.


The last 20 years have been dominated by an online disruption and optimization. That being said, I feel that the lives of creative people will continue to change drastically in the coming years. I want to enable a new creative revolution. With ArtBot.tv, we're building a new kind of content marketplace. A multimedia platform built by the people, for the people.

The Future

I am eager to see what the future holds! Decreasing production costs, AI, VR, globalization, faster internet, improved collaboration tools; we are entering exciting times and tech is tech is the backbone behind all of this. But as automation continues to grow, creative pursuits will also grow. We are entereing an era where tech and creativity will be more entertwined than ever. I'm determined to have ArtBot.tv be a major player in shaping the future of the independent digital content industry.
